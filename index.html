About the Center

An independent authority
for the age of financial intelligence

As financial institutions deploy AI agents for trading, risk assessment, advisory, and compliance, the need for a trusted, independent certification body has never been greater. GFAAC was established to fill that role — providing rigorous, transparent, and internationally recognized accreditation for financial AI agents.

Our multidisciplinary panels of financial experts, AI researchers, and regulators evaluate every agent against nine core standards before issuing a verifiable certification. Powered by advanced large language models, our certification intelligence platform delivers consistent, evidence-based assessments at global scale.

Integrity

Independent, evidence-based assessments free from commercial influence.

Global Scope

Standards harmonized across jurisdictions, from New York to Singapore.

Rigor

Nine-dimensional evaluation covering accuracy, safety, fairness, and auditability.

Innovation

AI-powered assessment engine keeps pace with rapidly evolving agent capabilities.

The Certification Journey

From application to accredited

A transparent, five-stage process designed for rigor without unnecessary friction.

01

Application

Submit your agent profile, intended use case, and supporting documentation through our portal.

02

Intake & Triage

Our AI engine performs an initial readiness scan and assigns your assessment panel.

03

Multi-Standard Evaluation

Rigorous testing across all nine standards — accuracy, robustness, fairness, security, and more.

04

Panel Review

Human experts review findings, request evidence, and render an accreditation decision.

05

Certification

Receive a verifiable digital certificate, a public registry entry, and a continuous-monitoring plan.
